Quenching Thirst, Pure and Simple!

Tripple T Oasis is a bottled drinking water vendor and refill business that provides high-quality, purified drinking water to customers who are concerned about the quality of their tap water. We offer a convenient and eco-friendly way for customers to obtain clean and safe drinking water.

Products & Services

what we offer

Our main product is purified drinking water, which we sell through our vending machines and refill stations. Our machines and stations use a state-of-the-art purification process that removes harmful contaminants and minerals from the water, leaving it clean and refreshing. Customers can bring their own refillable containers to our stations and fill them up with our processed water

In addition to our water products, we also offer a range of accessories, such as reusable water bottles, caps, and straws, to help customers reduce their environmental footprint and save money on single-use plastic bottles.

Mission

Driven Purpose

Our mission at Tripple T Oasis is to provide our customers with access to clean and healthy drinking water while reducing the negative impact of plastic waste on the environment. We are committed to providing a convenient and affordable service that promotes sustainability and health.

Management Team

Guiding Visionaries

Our management team has extensive experience in the drinking water industry and is committed to providing high-quality products and services to our customers.
